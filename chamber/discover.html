History of Syracuse

Syracuse, Utah, was founded in the late 1800s as a small farming community. Over the years, it has grown into a thriving city with a strong local economy and a rich cultural heritage.

Current Demographics

  • Population: ~35,500 residents
  • Household Income: $137,000
  • Median resident age: 29.2
  • Median House Value: ~$608,000
