Skylights Site Plan

Site Name

Sunlit Homes — The name is a blend of "Sun" and “Skylight”, representing the focus on natural lighting and its benefits in homes. It's short, brandable, and easy to remember.

Optional domain: sunlithomes.com (available for purchase)

Site Purpose

The site informs homeowners and builders about skylights — their benefits, types, installation guidance, maintenance, and how to request consultations.

Scenarios

  • Which type of skylight is best for my kitchen or bathroom?
  • How much does it cost to install a skylight in an existing roof?
  • Where can I find a local installer or schedule a consultation?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Navy Blue — Used for headings, buttons, and links.

Accent Color: Soft Yellow — Used for highlights, buttons, and background accents.

Typography

Font: Roboto

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body text: Roboto Regular
